St. Joseph's Degree College, located in Kurnool, India, is governed by St. Joseph's Educational Society and registered under the Societies Registration Act XXI of 1860 in 1967.
St. Joseph's Degree College is a self-financed college, without grant-in-aid.
t.Joseph's Degree College is one of the institutions run by St.Joseph's Educational Society.
Since its establishment, it has been progressing tremendously and today it is one of the premier institutions in the Rayalaseema districts. The goals and objectives of the institution in pursuit of excellence are: To provide a strong foundation to students for developing good theoretical and practical knowledge in their chosen areas. To build a strong interface with the industry for the skill development and the promotion of employability of the students, to fulfill the statement

"ENTER AS A STUDENT - LEAVE AS AN EMPLOYEE"

Focus on instilling a sense of discipline in the students. It is the ideal place for those who intend Graduate education, as we focus on the needs of each individual and aim to nurture each student in order to ensure their journey to future prospects. We are fortunate to have a talented, highly committed teaching and support staff to ensure the best learning environment. Delighted to have management which provides uncompromising infrastructural facilities. We encourage a three-way partnership between students, parents and the college. The strength of that partnership is reflected in the success of our students. It is my duty to ensure that the college remains for the students, as our founders envisioned it to be. I am fortunate to be in the position where I can listen to and solve the challenges that our students face, and make this learning experience just a little more valuable. I represent the diligent faculty and staff of our college, whose sincerity and spirit makes it what it is. It is my constant endeavor to flourish an environment of mutual respect within the college, in pursuit of a shared ambition.
